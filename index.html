The Water

What Makes It
Different.

Not all spring water is created equal. COOLER WATER™ comes from naturally alkaline springs — a source so rare, most people never taste it.

Pure & Pristine
Sourced from Protected
Underground Springs
Exceptionally Rare
Only 1% of springs in the United States are naturally alkaline. Most drinking water on the planet is neutral to acidic — finding a true naturally alkaline source is genuinely rare. pH 7.8
Discovered, Not Manufactured
Naturally alkaline water isn't processed or chemically adjusted. Its alkalinity comes from ancient rock and mineral-rich earth, filtered over time and not engineered in a facility.
Mineral-Rich by Nature
As water moves through layers of rock and sandstone in the Appalachian Mountains, it naturally absorbs magnesium, calcium, and potassium — minerals that occur from the earth itself. Not added. Not supplemented.
Canned at the Source
From spring to sealed can without compromise. Canning at the source means no long-haul transport, no exposure, and no loss of quality before it reaches your hands.
